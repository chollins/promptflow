Day 3: Hiking and Cultural Experiences in Seoraksan National Park

Early Morning

  • Breakfast: Fuel up at a local cafÃ©.
  • Travel: Drive to Seoraksan National Park (approximately a 2-hour drive).

Morning

  • Adventure Activity: Hike the Biryong Falls Trail or Ulsanbawi Rock Trail, depending on your fitness level. Both offer jaw-dropping views and rewarding experiences.
  • Tip: Start early to beat the crowds!

Afternoon

  • Lunch: Pack a picnic to enjoy in the park or eat at a local restaurant near the entrance.

Early Evening

  • Cultural Experience: Visit Sinheungsa Temple, located within the national park, to experience the serenity of Korean Buddhism.

Return to Seoul

  • Dinner: Drive back to Seoul and have dinner at Myeongdong Kyoja, known for its handmade knife-cut noodles.

Night

  • Optional: Explore Myeongdong Night Market for dessert and late-night shopping.

General Tips:

  • Car Rental: Ensure you have a GPS or offline maps to navigate around the cities and rural areas.
  • Currency: Keep cash handy for markets and small eateries as some places might not accept cards.
  • Local Etiquette: Always greet locals politely, and remember to bow as a sign of respect.
  • Language: Invest in a translation app, or learn a few basic Korean phrases for a smoother experience.
